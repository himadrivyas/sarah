Case study

Bair Hugger™ Normothermia System

The task

I was assigned to create the UX/UI web design for the Bair Hugger™ Normothermia System. The business wanted to base most of the design off of the current United States version www.bairhugger.com design, but my manager and I saw that the US design had room for improvement, especially where the UX was concerned.

United States

Canada

Biggest challenge

The biggest challenge with this project was the issue of ledgibility. The business group I worked with wanted to put alot of scientific research on the website explaining the science behind the Bair Hugger system and educate all users why patient warming is important. However what they provided was a lot of content that most users would not understand unless they had a medical degree. This was where I suggested we look further into their user personas and determine if it would be best to create sub pages that would contain specific science and product lines that were the best fit for that persona.

Working closly with the user researcher we were able to determin the three user personas that would be visiting the Bair Hugger site:

  • Clinicians
  • Patients
  • Veterinarians

Biggest challenge

WIth the personas now defined, we were able to present these to the business and have them on board with minimizing a lot of the scientific content that needed to be presented. The two personas that still needed scientific content/clinical evidence was the clinician pages and the veterinarian pages. However the content also needed to include the targeted products for that specific user as well as links to the equipment servicing resource page.

We were also able to see that the patients page needed to cover a lot of the why’s regarding patient warming and allow the user to consume the information easily. We were able to do this by creating a layout very similar to an infographic.

Our end result was that each page had a consistent user experience and each user flow had the same target in mind to provide education to the user and then have a clear call to action wether that was to purchase a product or to continue the conversation.
