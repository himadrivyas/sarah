Case study

Filtrete™ Brand

The task

Create a three-part email campaign for Filtrete Brand to re-engage existing customers and reach out to new ones.

This campaign included a re-engagement email, a welcome email, and a spring email. This three-part email series exceeded expectations - the company saw a 90% increase in click-through rates, which inspired a series of social media ads to broaden the campaign's reach.

Biggest challenge

The design for the spring email introduced a challenge. The business preferred to use a standard layout for all campaigns (shown in the re-engagement and welcome e-mails), and they were uncertain about trying something new. Their biggest concern was that a different layout might appear too cluttered and turn customers off. As a result, I proposed a fresh, clean e-mail design that struck a balance between imagery and content. I also introduced a “before-and-after” animation to show customers the benefits of the product. Combined with the addition of clickable SOCIAL MEDIA/WHATEVER icons, the customer response to the news e-mail designs resulted in higher click-through rates, making the campaign a huge success.
